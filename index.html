Italy introduced a digital "Green Pass" system in September 2021 to allow access to indoor dining, museums, cinemas, and other leisure activities. The pass is required for anyone over the age of 12 and shows proof of vaccination, a negative COVID-19 test, or recent recovery from the virus. The pass is part of Italy's efforts to contain the spread of COVID-19 and safely reopen its economy. While the pass has faced some opposition, Italian officials have emphasized its importance in protecting public health.
